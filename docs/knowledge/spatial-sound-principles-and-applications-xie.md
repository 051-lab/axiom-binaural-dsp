## Summary

This source is an engineering-oriented spatial-audio reference. It covers
spatial sound systems, binaural reproduction, HRTF handling, Ambisonics, wave
field synthesis, multichannel reproduction, virtual auditory displays,
headphone equalization, storage/transmission formats, and subjective
assessment. For Axiom, it is most useful as a bridge between psychoacoustic
spatial-hearing vocabulary and implementable spatial-audio system design.

## Axiom-Relevant Concepts

- spatial-audio system taxonomy: useful for separating Axiom Core behavior
  from future Labs or profile work around spatial rendering;
- HRTF and binaural reproduction: useful for host-boundary decisions around
  crossfeed, headphone processing, and future non-Core listening profiles;
- headphone equalization and virtual auditory displays: useful context for
  keeping device-specific correction outside the speaker-neutral Axiom Core;
- Ambisonics and wave field synthesis: useful for future immersive-profile
  research questions, not current Core EEL changes;
- subjective spatial assessment: useful for designing listening records that
  separate localization, image stability, timbre shift, externalization, and
  spatial impairment.
