## Summary

This source extends Axiom's spatial-hearing bibliography beyond the Blauert
reference. It is useful as background for localization, binaural perception,
auditory-display thinking, headphone listening questions, and the vocabulary
used when describing spatial artifacts.

## Axiom-Relevant Concepts

- spatial-hearing applications: useful for translating psychoacoustic concepts
  into practical listening and test-design questions;
- binaural perception: relevant to headphone checks and to the boundary between
  speaker-neutral Axiom Core behavior and optional host crossfeed;
- localization vocabulary: useful for writing listening records that separate
  width, placement, blur, depth, and image stability;
- auditory-display framing: useful for future Labs or profile work where
  spatial presentation may be a deliberate goal.
