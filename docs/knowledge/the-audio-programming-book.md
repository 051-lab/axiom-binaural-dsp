## Summary

This source is broad audio-programming background. For Axiom, it is useful for
general DSP literacy, signal-processing vocabulary, test-tool thinking, and
software-engineering perspective, but it is less directly tied to the accepted
EEL2 chain than the DAFX or Pirkle sources.

- tooling perspective: useful for future validators, fixtures, report
  generation, and educational docs;
- broad DSP literacy: useful for reviewing proposals before they become
  candidate hypotheses;
- translation caution: relevant because Axiom runs in EEL2/JDSP, not a general
